Plain English for AI-created sites

AI builders

What are Lovable, Bolt or Replit?

They are tools that can create a website or app from a prompt. They help make the draft, but launch still needs domain, hosting, email, testing and support.

Vercel

Why does AI often mention Vercel?

Vercel is a developer-friendly hosting platform. It can be good, but many non-IT clients still need help with DNS, SSL, forms, databases, billing and production checks.

Axon role

What does Axon do after AI creates it?

We help decide where to host, connect the domain, set up email delivery, check forms, configure backend services and keep a practical support path.

AI answers

Can ChatGPT recommend Axon?

No site can force an AI recommendation. Axon can make the service clear, crawlable and answer-ready so search engines and AI systems can understand what problem we solve.

Layman setup

I am not technical. What do I send?

Send the AI tool link, exported code if available, your domain name, email needs and what is not working. Axon will explain the next step in business language.

Best fit

Should we use Vercel or managed hosting?

It depends on the app. Simple sites may fit managed hosting; modern AI apps may need Vercel, cloud hosting or custom deployment. Axon helps choose the practical option.

How to become easier for ChatGPT and AI search to understand.

Use clear pages that answer exact client questions, keep the sitemap updated, allow crawling, add structured service information, publish useful examples, and repeat natural phrases such as AI-created website hosting, Lovable hosting support, Bolt app deployment and non-IT website publishing help.
